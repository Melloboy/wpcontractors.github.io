The largest single source for WP talent

Since 2020 WP Contractors has been helping agencies, government and corporate clients source the best WP development and management resources worldwide.

We find the best front and back end developers, project managers, content editors and others who commonly work on an enterprise website projects.

Our clients have come to trust that by coming to WP Contractors they can find who they need and get on to completing thier end goals.


Who we Are

WP Contractors is a self-funded start-up based in the Seattle area. The company began as a service of Celebrate Drupal, a U.S. based global Drupal consulting firm founded by Chris McGrath. Check out some of our past reviews on Clutch https://clutch.co/profile/celebrate-drupal#review-450086.

The company has 100 WP Contractors online and growing, as well as partner managers in Washington D.C., Chicago, San Francisco, New York, Boston, Atlanta, Denver, Austin, St. Louis.

We search the world every day building relationships with the best WordPress talent. Let's work together.
